The pronouns she and her are replacing the nouns Anna and Rico.
Like all other words, nouns can be singular or plural. Also, there are three types of nouns: subjective, objective and possessive.

Subjective:

These are pronouns which are the subject of the sentence . They are also used if they rename the subject.
Example: ______ did well on the test.
I, You, he, she, we, they …all qualify to fill in the blank

Objective:

These are pronouns which are the object of the sentence.
Example: The teacher told _____ to stand.
me, you, him, her, us, them …all qualify to fill in the blank

Possessive:

These are pronouns which are used to express ownership.
Example: The red bag is _______.
Mine, yours, his, hers, ours, theirs …all qualify to fill in the blank
